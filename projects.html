    In my UX portfolio, I've chosen to showcase a diverse selection of projects that highlight my expertise in creating intuitive and effective user experiences. The portfolio includes UX research for a coding app aimed at children, which involved unique challenges in making complex concepts accessible to young learners.

    Additionally, it features UX/UI design for a precision measuring app, demonstrating my ability to design detailed, user-friendly interfaces for technical applications. The third project is a UX design for a cryptocurrency custodial wallet, reflecting my skills in securing and simplifying financial transactions in the digital space.

    While my career has spanned various domains, including food delivery, dating, and gambling management apps, I selected these three projects for their distinctive challenges and the opportunity they provided to innovate within diverse application areas. This selection underscores my versatility and commitment to enhancing user interaction across different technology platforms.
