    • Custodial Crypto Wallet

    Overview

    Client/Company: Private Client

    Duration: October 2023 — Present

    Tools Used: Figma, Phyton for data analysis, various UX research methods.

    Role: UX Researcher/Designer, website developer (HTML, CSS, JS)

    Objective/Problem Statement

    The goal is to develop a secure, intuitive custodial cryptocurrency wallet app that simplifies the management and transaction of digital assets while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. The app will feature robust security measures, support multiple cryptocurrencies, and provide a user-friendly interface suitable for both beginners and experienced users.

    User Persona: Emily Sharp

    Age:

    29

    Location:

    San Francisco, CA

    Education:

    Bachelor's Degree in Fine Arts

    Occupation:

    Graphic Designer

    Background

    Moderately tech-savvy; comfortable using smartphones and apps but not deeply knowledgeable about cryptocurrency technology. Prefers using apps that provide a balance of simplicity and functionality. Values customer support highly and often reads FAQs and help articles before reaching out for help.

    Goals

    To securely buy, store, and occasionally trade cryptocurrencies without dealing with the technical complexities of non-custodial wallets and have a simple and intuitive app interface that allows easy monitoring of investments and straightforward transaction processes.

    Challenges

    Intimidated by the perceived risks and complexities of managing cryptographic keys herself. Needs reassurance of security measures to protect her investments against fraud and theft.

    Quote

    "I want to invest in crypto without becoming a tech expert."

    App Benefits

    The custodial cryptocurrency wallet app provides Emily, a casual crypto enthusiast, with a straightforward and secure way to manage her digital assets. Its intuitive design simplifies her investment process, while robust security features keep her investments safe, offering peace of mind. Additionally, readily available customer support ensures she can get help whenever she needs it, making her crypto journey smooth and stress-free.

    User Persona: Raj Sahjani

    Age:

    42

    Location:

    New York City, NY

    Education:

    MBA in Finance

    Occupation:

    Finance Consultant

    Background

    Highly knowledgeable about both technology and finance; already uses multiple investment platforms and tools. Engages regularly with financial news and updates to stay ahead in the market. Prefers platforms that offer detailed analytics and the ability to automate certain transactions.

    Goals

    To diversify his investment portfolio by including a variety of cryptocurrencies and use advanced features like staking, lending, and earning interest to maximize returns.

    Challenges

    Needs a platform that offers high levels of security and compliance with financial regulations. Requires real-time data and alerts to stay informed about market changes and opportunities.

    Quote

    "I need a tool that matches my investment strategies with top-tier security and insightful analytics."

    App Benefits

    The custodial cryptocurrency wallet app caters to Raj's needs as a savvy investor by offering a platform that aligns with his advanced investment strategies and desire for top-tier security. It supports a variety of cryptocurrencies and includes features like staking, lending, and earning interest, enabling him to diversify and grow his portfolio efficiently. The app also provides real-time data and market alerts, keeping him informed and responsive to market movements. Furthermore, its adherence to regulatory standards reassures him that his investments are both secure and compliant with financial regulations. This combination of functionality, security, and compliance makes the app an ideal tool for managing his sophisticated crypto investments.
